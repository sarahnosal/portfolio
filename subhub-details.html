SubHub

  • Category: Single Page Web App
  • Client: Flatiron Phase 4 Project
  • Project date: 16 February, 2022
  • Project URL: subhub-kg-sn.herokuapp.com
  • Github: Phase4_SubHub_app
  • Demo: Watch demo here!

Track your subscriptions and see a breakdown of monthly expenses.

This app was made to fix the problem of not knowing when your subscriptions are coming out of your account each month. You can add or delete subscriptions and the app will show you a breakdown of your monthly expenses as well as a calendar that shows which expenses are charged on which day. Read about the technical aspect below:

  • Built an authentication system from scratch to store user information and secure passwords.
  • Created an extensive backend through Ruby on Rails with full CRUD allowing users to create subscriptions in the app, as well as update different aspects of those subscriptions.
  • Incorporated Material UI to display data in a pie-chart with data from a SQL database being pulled in real-time.
